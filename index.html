HOW IT WORKS

Get cash for your items when they sell! Bring up to 15 gently used clothing or household items during intake hours. If you would like to bring more than 15 items, please call to schedule an appointment with us. Our consignment specialists will check over your items to ensure they meet the standards our customers demand.

About Us

Nice As New was founded in 1984 in a
small shop on Wausau's West side. We
have grownsteadily over the last 25+
years to become central Wisconsin's
best resale store and your source for
high quality, gently used clothing and
household items. Inventory changes daily,
so shop often because you won't find our
vast selection of clothing and household
items anywhere else!
