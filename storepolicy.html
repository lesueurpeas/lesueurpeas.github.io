Store Policy

Store Policy

  • Please make and appointment if you have more than 15 items of intake.
  • Nice As new is a seasonal consignment store. Please refer to our Intake Schedule for the best time to bring your merchandise in.
  • Clothing should be no more than three years old and in like new condition, free of any stains, tears, broken zippers, missing buttoms, and any smoke or musty smell.
  • All toys and small appliances should be in working order witha ll the necessary parks, owners manual, and any remote control or other accessories needed to properly work.
  • You will recieve forty percent of what your items sell for.
  • To protect your account, we request a drivers license or photo ID to collect your money.
  • You will recieve cash/check for any items sold.
  • Nice As New reserves the right to offer sales, coupons, and discounts on any merchandise.
  • Items are on the selling floor for about two months at full price, and then reduced to 50% for two weeks.
  • Not all items will sell. If you would like your items back it is your responsibility to find them int he sore. Nice As New will not contact you. Any items left after the alloted time will become property of Nice As New LLC and will be donated to charity, liquidated, or discarded.
  • We try to price your items at the best possible price, however, if you have an item you would like to discuss a specific price for, please let us know before you leave the items for resale.
  • Pleace check the Consumer Product Safety Commision website ( www.cpsc.gov ) for recall information on any infant and childrens toys, and any equipment you wish to sell.
  • We actively work with law enforcement in retrieving stolen merchandise. You could be subjecct to criminal penalties for bringing in stole items to resell. By bringing in items you attest under penatly of law that you are the rightful owner or have legal authority to sell the items.
