About Us

Nice As New was founded in 1984 in a small shop on Wausau's West side. We have grown steadily over the last 25+ years to become central Wisconsin's best resale store and your source for high quality, gently used clothing and household items. Inventory changes daily, so shop often because you won't find our vast selection of clothing and household items anywhere else!
